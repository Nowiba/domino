Domino Game Rules

General Domino Rules

Dominoes are rectangular tiles with two square ends, each marked with a number of spots (pips) or blank.

Game Modes

Block Dominoes

  • Players take turns matching dominoes end to end
  • If a player cannot play, they pass their turn
  • The first player to run out of dominoes wins
  • If the game is blocked, the player with the fewest dominoes wins

Draw Dominoes

  • Similar to Block, but players draw from the boneyard when they can't play
  • The boneyard is exhausted when only 2 dominoes remain

All Fives

  • Players score points when the open ends add up to a multiple of five
  • Each multiple of five is worth that many points (5=5, 10=10, etc.)
  • The first player to reach the target score wins

Mexican Train

  • Players build their own "train" of dominoes
  • Doubles must be covered before other plays can be made
  • Special rules apply when a double is played
